# Writing Prompt Response
To make park benches facilitate more social interaction, I would first consider how a bench is designed.
If the goal is to make the park more social, each bench will be designed to allow multiple parkgoers to rest on them for extended periods of time.
Thus, each bench will be built wide enough to allow up to three parkgoers to rest in addition to back support to prevent discomfort.
In terms of placement, benches will be stationed around park equipment and points of interest, such as playgrounds, exercise stations, etc.
The placement of the benches increases general accessibility and would draw more parkgoers to each point of interest.
Furthermore, the benches would encourage socialization while resting depending on the structure they are placed near.
For example, parents are given the opportunity to socialize while their kids play in a jungle gym.
Overall, the design of the benches encourages guests to rest while the placement of the benches around structures opens up opportunities to socialize
